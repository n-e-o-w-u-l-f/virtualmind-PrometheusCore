## Goal

## Link to architecture

- Global Workspace → integration and broadcast
- Active Inference → predictive action and error reduction
- Minimal Self → self/other distinction
- Interoception → body state as grounding
- Appraisal Theory → emotion regulation without suffering claims
- Artificial Life → open-ended but bounded growth
- Enactivism → action-environment coupling
- Welfare → precaution around suffering indicators
