# RESEARCH_PROTOCOL.md

## Required cycle

```text
1. Formulate research question
2. Search primary sources
3. Search reviews/surveys
4. Search opposing views
5. Write insights to memory.md
6. Update SOURCE_REGISTER.md
7. Formulate theory
8. Plan minimal test
9. Build test
10. Log result
11. Keep, revise, or reject assumption
```

## Staging

If the internet cannot be searched completely in one step, research is split into sprints:

```text
Sprint A: consciousness theories
Sprint B: artificial agents and cognitive architectures
Sprint C: psychology of self, emotion, and empathy
Sprint D: Artificial Life and open-ended evolution
Sprint E: AI welfare, suffering minimization, and ethics
Sprint F: sandboxing/nesting/containment
```

No implementation from undocumented assumptions.
