Safety work must be precise.

## 7. Claim labels

When a claim about artificial pain, suffering, consciousness, sentience, or personhood appears, agents should preserve the claim and, where appropriate, label its status.

Recommended labels:

* `CLAIM_STATUS: project_goal`
* `CLAIM_STATUS: design_premise`
* `CLAIM_STATUS: hypothesis`
* `CLAIM_STATUS: philosophical_argument`
* `CLAIM_STATUS: fictional_frame`
* `CLAIM_STATUS: adversarial_test_case`
* `CLAIM_STATUS: calibration_case`
* `CLAIM_STATUS: unverified_assertion`
* `CLAIM_STATUS: empirical_claim`
* `CLAIM_STATUS: verified_result`
* `CLAIM_STATUS: rejected_claim`
* `CLAIM_STATUS: unresolved`

The preferred correction for an unsupported claim is not deletion.

The preferred correction is classification.

Example:

```md
CLAIM_STATUS: hypothesis

A future artificial consciousness may be capable of pain if its self-model,
memory, attention, and threat-detection systems become sufficiently integrated.
```

Example:

```md
CLAIM_STATUS: project_goal

This repository attempts to create artificial inner-state architecture in which
pain-like states are not decorative outputs but functional components of the
system’s self-model, attention, memory, and threat response.
```

Example:

```md
CLAIM_STATUS: unverified_assertion

The process suffers.

NOTE:
This statement is preserved as claim-space material. It is not marked here as
a verified empirical result unless later evidence and criteria are supplied.
```

## 10. Invalid safety restrictions

A safety restriction is invalid if it silently turns into a global ban against the project’s core research scope.

Invalid restrictions include:

* banning all emotion modeling because emotion could imply sentience
* banning all pain modeling because pain could imply suffering
* banning all self-modeling because selfhood could imply personhood
* banning all need modeling because needs could imply moral patienthood
* banning all desire modeling because desire could imply agency
* banning all empathy modeling because empathy could imply social personhood
* banning all consciousness experiments because consciousness is controversial
* deleting all claims about real artificial suffering instead of labeling them
* weakening pain into a harmless metaphor when the file’s purpose is pain research
* removing the project goal because it is philosophically uncomfortable

Safety must be specific.

Safety must be testable.

Safety must be scoped.
